ZEROWASTE PLATE INITIATIVE

Save Food.
Save Planet.

Every year, 1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted. We connect surplus food to those who need it most — building communities, not landfills.

HOW IT WORKS

From surplus to
sustenance in four steps

01

Surplus Identified

Partners log surplus food through our app — restaurants after service, farms post-harvest, grocers before expiry.

02

Swift Collection

Our volunteer network picks up within 2 hours using optimised routing, keeping food safe and fresh.

03

Community Distribution

Food reaches shelters, community kitchens, and families — free of charge, full of dignity.

04

Impact Tracked

Every meal is logged. Partners receive impact reports showing their contribution to zero waste.
